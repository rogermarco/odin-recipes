Scones

If you're able to wrestle it all together on the table, verses mixing into a dough ball in the bowl, you'll be rewarded with a much tenderer scone.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Poppy seeds
  • Sugar
  • Lemon zest
  • Egg
  • Lemon juice
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix everything into a dough
  2. Form into rectangle and cut
  3. Bake
